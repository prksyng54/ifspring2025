Banana Bread Recipe

Description

This classic banana bread is moist, flavorful, and easy to make with simple pantry ingredients. It's the perfect way to use overripe bananas, creating a deliciously soft and slightly sweet loaf. Enjoy it as a breakfast treat, snack, or dessert with a cup of coffee or tea.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
